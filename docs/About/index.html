Jerrell Nolen

Overview

Just in case you couldn't see from the 100 other places it's plastered on the sight, my name is Jerrell Nolen. I am a Game Developer, currently enrolled as a student at Columbia College Chicago earning a BA in Game Development, with a focus in Narrative Development. I've worked on two major projects at the moment including a game entitled Installation 23 and another called Project Steampunk. I have co-founded a company called Kappa Korp LLC, which Project Steampunk is being created under. I also built this website entirely.

Extra

It's fairly easy to assume, but one of my favorite things to do is play games, outside of that I absolutely love music of close to all genres, and working with people of other creative backgrounds. I have a lot of friends that produce and write music so I enjoy bringing them together to collaborate and work.

Gearbox Software,

Borderlands

The Borderlands game series saw its first launch in October of 2009, bringing the first RPGFPS game to light. It paved the way for all looter shooter games to follow with 4 playable characters with expansive skill trees and a plethora of unique and interesting weapons.

Borderlands 2

The second game released within the Borderlands series, Borderlands 2 released in September of 2012, continuing what it had previously started by expanding upon the already made universe. The PG-13 to R rated comedy became more prevalent and more stylistic, weapon types became more defined along with adding more unique weapons and implementing a new boss system. Everything the first game did, this one improved upon.

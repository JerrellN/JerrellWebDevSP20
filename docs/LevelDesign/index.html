Megaman Maker: Underground Cavern Map

This leads to a video of a custom map I made using the Megaman Maker engine. The map was created to be a single, short but cohesive experience gradually growing more difficult as you progressed. The early section of the map is more easy, allowing the player to adjust to only having the sword to attack with, enemies being more simple to deal with. As progression continues, the challenges grow, culiminating with a boss fight to test the skills gained through the level.

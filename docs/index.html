Overview

I am Jerrell Nolen, a Game Developer, an alumni of Columbia College Chicago with a BA in Game Development, with focuses in Narrative Development and Level Design . I've worked on platforming action adventure game for Ubisoft (unreleased), and two indie games that are both incomplete. One for Kappa Korp, atop down 2D rpg , and the other for Team Cat RPG, a heavily narrative based RPG.

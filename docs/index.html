Overview

Just in case you couldn't see from the 100 other places it's plastered on the sight, my name is Jerrell Nolen. I am a Game Developer, an alumni of Columbia College Chicago with a BA in Game Development, with focuses in Narrative Development and Level Design. I've worked on two major projects at the moment including a game entitled Installation 23 and another called Project Steampunk, and I have co-founded a company called Kappa Korp LLC, which Project Steampunk is being created under. Fun Fact: I also built this website entirely from scratch.
